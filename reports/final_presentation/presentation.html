1 · Problem & Answer ~2 min

The system flags everyone. We can only investigate 50.

How might NordikBank turn 15,000 noise alerts into 50 high-confidence investigations?
2 / 6
Capacity

15,000 alerts/month. 45 analysts. ~50 deep investigations. The math doesn't work.

Signal quality

97% false positive rate. Static rules can't tell a cash business from a structuring scheme.

Regulatory pressure

FSA review Q3 2025. Post-Danske Bank, explainable risk-based monitoring is mandatory.

Blind spots

Rules evaluate transactions in isolation — no behavioural history, no cross-signal synthesis.

Cost of misses

Under 6AMLD, the bank is liable for missed SAR filings.

⬡ Better model — rank risk, don't flag everything
  • 64 behavioural features from raw transactions — velocity, structuring, counterparty, geography
  • XGBoost customer-level score: AUC-ROC 0.889 vs. 0.73 rule-based baseline (+22%)
  • Per-customer feature contributions — every score has a receipt
⬡ Better analysts — prevent overwork, enable focus
  • Prioritised alert queue — highest-risk customers first, no random triage
  • Customer 360 view — full behavioural profile, top risk drivers, transaction history
  • Capacity-aligned threshold — queue always within the team's reach
Our edge is not the model alone — it is the balance between technical performance and analyst wellbeing. Explainability is the bridge between ML and compliance.
